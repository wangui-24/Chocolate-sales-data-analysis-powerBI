**Chocolate-sales-data-analysis-powerBI
 Chocolate Sales Performance Analysis**
 
**1. Project Overview**
This project analyzes chocolate sales data to uncover trends in revenue, growth, product performance, country performance, and salesperson effectiveness.  
An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was built to provide both executive-level insights and detailed performance analysis.

The goal of the project is to demonstrate **end-to-end business intelligence skills** ‚Äî from data modeling and DAX calculations to dashboard design and data storytelling.
**

**2**. Business Objective****
- Monitor overall sales performance and growth
- Measure **Year-over-Year (YoY)** sales trends
- Identify top-performing products, countries, and salespeople
- Understand how volume (boxes shipped) aligns with revenue
- Enable data-driven decision-making for sales and market strategy


**3. Dataset Description**
The dataset consists of cleaned sales data with the following fields:
- Date  
- Product  
- Country  
- Salesperson  
- Sales Amount  
- Boxes Shipped  
**
4. Data Modeling Approach**
- Created a **dedicated Date Table** to support accurate time intelligence analysis
- Established a **one-to-many relationship** between the Date Table and Sales table
- Marked the Date Table as the official date table in Power BI
- Ensured best practices for scalability and performance

---
****
5. Key Metrics (DAX Measures)
- **Total Sales**
- **Total Boxes Shipped**
- **Sales Last Year**
- **Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth %**

These measures enable consistent performance comparison across years and dimensions.

**---

6. Dashboard Features**
 KPI Cards
- Total Sales  
- YoY Sales Growth (%)  
- Total Boxes Shipped  

**7. Visualizations**
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Sales by Country
- Top 5 Salespeople by Sales
- Salesperson-by-Country performance table with YoY %
- Interactive slicers and drill-through for detailed analysis

---
**8. Key Insights**
- Sales increased consistently from **2022 through 2024**, indicating sustained growth
- Revenue growth closely mirrors volume growth, suggesting stable pricing
- A small group of products contributes a significant share of total revenue
- Sales performance is concentrated in a few key countries
- Top salespeople drive a disproportionate share of overall sales

---

**9.Business Impact**
- Enables leadership to quickly identify revenue drivers
- Supports strategic decisions around product focus and market expansion
- Highlights top-performing salespeople and regions
- Provides a scalable reporting framework for future analysis
